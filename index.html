My Journey

I am someone who has personally faced the relentless challenges of insomnia. Enduring sleepless nights throughout my college years, I found myself in a seemingly endless cycle of fatigue and frustration. The futility I felt, the hopelessness of not being able to sleep—it was an agonizing experience. I would have done anything, paid any price, just to experience a night of restful sleep again. It was during this tumultuous period that I embarked on a journey to find a solution. Through my own struggles, I discovered the transformative power of CBT-I. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia (CBT-I) is a structured, evidence-based approach aimed at improving sleep by addressing thoughts, behaviors, and habits that may contribute to insomnia. Unlike medications, CBT-I focuses on developing healthy sleep patterns and changing behaviors that hinder restful sleep. It often involves techniques such as sleep restriction, stimulus control, cognitive restructuring, and relaxation strategies. CBT-I is known for its effectiveness in treating insomnia and is a key component of my sleep coaching approach. If you have specific questions about CBT-I or how it may benefit you, feel free to discuss it during your initial consultation.
