About Us

Hello and Welcome!

If you're here, you likely understand the profound impact that quality sleep has on our overall well-being. In a world that constantly demands our attention and energy, the importance of a good night's sleep cannot be overstated. It's not merely about the number of hours we spend in bed; it's about the rejuvenation and restoration that occurs during those precious moments of rest. Quality sleep is the cornerstone of a healthy and balanced life.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Behavioral Neuroscience and Psychology, and I am currently completing my M.D. (Doctor of Medicine). I have also recently completed a certification in CBT-I (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia). During my ongoing medical training, I've worked in numerous hospitals and clinics across various countries. It's become evident to me that many healthcare professionals face significant challenges, such as limited time and resources, when addressing insomnia—a crucial aspect of overall health that often goes unaddressed.

My Journey

I am someone who has personally faced the relentless challenges of insomnia. Enduring sleepless nights throughout my college years, I found myself in a seemingly endless cycle of fatigue and frustration. The futility I felt, the hopelessness of not being able to sleep—it was an agonizing experience. I would have done anything, paid any price, just to experience a night of restful sleep again. It was during this tumultuous period that I embarked on a journey to find a solution. Through my own struggles, I discovered the transformative power of CBT-I. It not only transformed my sleep but also inspired me to help others on their path to restful nights.

Let's Begin Your Sleep Transformation

Are you ready to say goodbye to sleepless nights? Whether you're grappling with insomnia, seeking to improve sleep hygiene, or simply yearning for a more restful slumber, I am here for you. Feel free to reach out to schedule a free consultation. Let's embark on this journey together, drawing from both scientific knowledge and personal experience, to unlock the full potential of your sleep.
